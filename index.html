About

Why RAAESF-2026

A global platform hosted by the Department of Agricultural Engineering, REVA University to accelerate innovation in sustainable farming.

RAAESF-2026 convenes academicians, researchers, industry professionals, and students to discuss recent advances in Agricultural Engineering. The conference emphasizes collaboration across disciplines to advance knowledge, technology, and implementation in an evolving agri-tech landscape. Selected papers will be published in Scopus indexed and UGC journals.

Publication partners include Scopus-indexed Q-rated journals; publication charges are borne by authors.

Themes

Conference Tracks

Explore the four pillars shaping sustainable farming.

Farm Mechanization & Automation

  • Tillage and traction machinery
  • Planting, transplanting, protection, harvesting, threshing
  • Precision agriculture technologies
  • Agricultural drones, AI, robotics, mechatronics
  • Ergonomics and safety in farm operations

Post-Harvest & Secondary Agriculture

  • Sensor-based post-harvest engineering
  • Emerging food processing technologies
  • Dairy, meat, poultry, fish, fiber processing
  • Utilization and value addition of by-products

Land & Water Technologies

  • Geospatial resource management
  • Aquifer and groundwater sustainability
  • AI, IoT, ML for efficient land and water management
  • Smart watershed monitoring and modeling
  • Vertical farming and protected cultivation

Renewable & Green Energy

  • Renewable energy in agriculture and agro-industries
  • Solar-powered agricultural technologies
  • Biomass energy and biofuel production
  • Clean, green energy systems and transition

The university offers 37 undergraduate, 28 postgraduate, and 19 Ph.D. programmes across Engineering, Architecture, Science and Technology, Commerce, Management, Law, Arts & Humanities, and Performing Arts.
